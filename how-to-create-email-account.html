How to Create an Email Account

Your email account is something you most likely take for granted. In all probability, one of the first things you did when you connected to the internet for the first time was to open a browser and establish a new inbox. For your day-to-day personal tasks, the free email services provided by the likes of Google and Yahoo are adequate, but if you want to build a website and establish an online presence, you need to have a branded email address that is associated with your domain name.

Table of Contents
  • 1. Why Branded Emails are Crucial for Businesses
  • 2. Creating Email Accounts in CloudHoste Panel
  • 3. Accessing Your New Mailbox (Webmail & Desktop Clients)
  • 4. Conclusion

Why Branded Emails are Crucial for Businesses

For businesses, branding is incredibly important. Let’s say you’ve created a great-looking online shop, and you’ve done all the marketing work. You’ve managed to attract potential clients, but they then see that your contact email is at Yahoo or Gmail. While it’s not a guaranteed turn-off, this may very well make them walk away.

Even if you’re a blogger that doesn’t really want to make heaps of money from your website, you are still better off using a branded email address as your main contact. Otherwise, your followers could be left with the impression that their emails might go unnoticed in the midst of the personal communication on your personal accounts.

Creating Email Accounts in CloudHoste Panel

Thankfully, if you have a registered domain and a hosting account, creating a branded email address is straightforward. Users of CloudHoste’s managed VPS hosting solutions can set up unlimited mailboxes (subject to VPS plan boundaries) using the CloudHoste Panel control panel. Here is what you need to do:

Step 1
Log Into Panel

Log into the CloudHoste Panel User Interface and locate the Email Accounts tool under the Email section on the homepage.

Step 2
Configure Details

Enter a username and pick your domain from the dropdown. Enter a strong password (minimum 8 characters, containing uppercase, numbers, and symbols) or use the generator tool.

Step 3
Save Inbox

Click the Create Email button to save changes. Your new mailbox will be generated instantly and will appear in the table listing below the form.

Accessing Your New Mailbox (Webmail & Desktop Clients)

Once created, you can access your email using two main methods:

Webmail Interface

Access your inbox directly from any web browser by navigating to https://webmail.yourdomain.com and logging in with the full email address and password.

Desktop & Mobile Clients

Configure tools like Outlook, Apple Mail, or Thunderbird using the secure SSL/TLS server settings detailed in your CloudHoste welcome emails.

Through the Existing Email Accounts list, you can perform administrative actions like deleting legacy accounts, viewing log statistics, or changing passwords.

Conclusion

Creating a professional, branded email address boosts visitor confidence and aligns with industry security baselines. If you require help setting up MX records or configuring local email software, contact the CloudHoste support team for 24/7 technical assistance.
